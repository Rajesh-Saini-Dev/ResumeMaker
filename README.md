ResumeMaker:<br>
A modern, customizable resume builder built with React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS. Instantly create, preview, and download your resume as a PDF.

Features:<br>
Live resume editing with instant preview
Add, edit, and remove experiences, education, and skills
Download your resume as a PDF (using html2canvas and jsPDF)
Responsive and clean UI with Tailwind CSS

Usage:<br>
Fill in your personal details, experience, education, and skills in the form.<br>
Instantly see your resume preview update.<br>
Click Download PDF to save your resume.

Tech Stack:<br>
React Vite,<br>
Tailwind CSS,<br>
html2canvas,<br>
jsPDF
